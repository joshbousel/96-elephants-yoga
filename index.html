Why Yogis and Elephants?

We believe that the yoga community and conservationists can be strong allies in the campaign to save elephants from extinction. The yoga community’s values — including caring for and respecting all beings — align well with our conservation mission. And elephants need our help! The global demand for their ivory has fed a brutal slaughter, which claims 96 of them everyday. If we all work together, we can save elephants for future generations.
