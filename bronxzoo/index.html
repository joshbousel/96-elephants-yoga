Yogis for Ellies

RSVP for FREE yoga at the Bronx Zoo!

On Friday, September 23 from 4-7 p.m., we will host our first Yogis For Ellies event at the Bronx Zoo. Tao Porchon-Lynch, Kristin McGee, and Bethany Lyons will lead FREE yoga classes in honor of the 96 elephants who are lost every day for their ivory. Invite a friend and help us raise awareness for elephants.

Additional event details:

  • Free admission and parking.
  • Open animal exhibits including Madagascar!, Zoo Center, and Sea Lions with a special feeding.
  • Free snacks and refreshments.
  • This event is for adults only.
  • RSVP required by September 21.
  • BYO yoga mat!
